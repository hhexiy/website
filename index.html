I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. I work in the CLIP lab with my advisors Hal Daumé III and Jordan Boyd-Graber (at UC Boulder now). My interests are at the interface between machine learning and natural language processing. I develop algorithms that acquire information dynamically and do inference incrementally, with an emphasis on problems in natural language processing.
