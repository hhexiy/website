I am a post-doc researcher at Stanford University, working with Percy Liang. Prior to Stanford, I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park, advised by Hal Daumé III and Jordan Boyd-Graber (at CU Boulder now). My interests are at the interface between machine learning and natural language processing. I develop algorithms that acquire information dynamically and do inference incrementally, with an emphasis on problems in natural language processing.
